About Me
My Journey

My name is Boris Johnson, and I'm 15 years old. I was diagnosed with Muscular Dystrophy when I was just 10 years old. It was a challenging moment for me and my family, but we've learned to face each day with hope and resilience. Friends of Muscular Dystrophy Kenya has been instrumental in my journey, providing support and encouragement every step of the way.

Facing Challenges

Living with Muscular Dystrophy means adapting to physical limitations, but it hasn't dampened my spirit. School has been both rewarding and demanding. There are days when my muscles feel weaker than others, but my determination to succeed academically remains strong. I've learned that challenges are just opportunities to grow stronger mentally and emotionally. The support from FOMDK has helped me access better healthcare and educational resources.

Hobbies & Interests

When I'm not in school, I love spending time with my family and friends. I'm passionate about reading – it takes me to different worlds and lets me escape into amazing stories. I also enjoy playing chess, which helps me think strategically about life. Recently, I've discovered a love for digital art and graphic design. Creating things on my computer allows me to express my creativity without physical limitations.

My Dreams

My biggest dream is to become a software engineer. I want to use technology to create solutions that help people, especially those facing health challenges like myself. I believe that with the right education and support, nothing is impossible. I'm grateful for the opportunity to continue my studies and work towards making my dreams a reality. The future looks bright, and I'm ready to embrace it!

My Talents

What I'm Good At

  • Academic Excellence - Strong in Mathematics and Science subjects
  • Strategic Thinking - Chess enthusiast with problem-solving skills
  • Digital Design - Graphic design and creative visual composition
  • Communication - Strong verbal and written expression skills
  • Programming Basics - Learning Python and Web Development
  • Empathy - Ability to understand and support others
  • Teamwork - Collaborative spirit and group project leadership
  • Creativity - Innovative thinking and problem-solving
