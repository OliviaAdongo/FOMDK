Let's Bring Hope

Through your generous donations, Friends of Muscular Dystrophy Kenya (FOMDK) helps improve the quality of life for individuals and families affected by muscular dystrophy. Your support enables us to provide care, raise awareness, and build a stronger, more inclusive community for those living with this condition.

Donate via M-Pesa

  1. Open M-Pesa on your phone
  2. Select “Lipa na M-Pesa”
  3. Choose “Business Number”
  4. Enter Business No: 181381
  5. Enter Account Ref: FOMDK Donation
  6. Enter Amount: KES 1000
  7. Enter M-Pesa PIN
